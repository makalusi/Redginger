— Our Story

PASSION
ON EVERY
PLATE

Red Ginger was born from a single belief — that bold Asian flavours and fine dining belong together. Our head chef spent over a decade training across Tokyo, Bangkok, and Hong Kong before bringing that knowledge home.

Every dish on our menu is a conversation between tradition and creativity. We source the freshest local produce, pair it with authentic Asian technique, and plate it with an attention to detail that turns every meal into a memory.
